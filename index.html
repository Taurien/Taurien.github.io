About Hi, I'm Michel. Graphic designer and web developer in process. I'm a talented and dedicated person in everything I do.
I consider myself curious, multidisciplinary and easy to adapt to changes.

Usually I look for several ways to solve a problem, just to learn and maybe apply some of them to another project or problem.

I have a huge interest in the visual world, its order and the colors it can possess. Every day we are exposed to countless objects and stimuli, which many of them we do not detail. In my mind I make the exercise of trying to detail them all and observe the use of color, its layout, its relationship with respect to other objects or us as users, what influences it has and why, and thousands other questions that cross my mind.

This way of observing the world is what led me to choose design as a career and thanks to it I have been able to expand my judgment of what I observe and how many things are developed.

Recently I chose to immerse myself in the Web world, because the questions I ask myself and the basics are the same. Therefore, I'm daily learning and retaking the subject, to achieve the goal of being an IU/IX designer and web developer.
